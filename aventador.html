Overview

The legend can not be re-engraved, only constantly challenged. Therefore, Aventador always insists on breaking through self. Following Miura, Islero, Countach and Urraco, in the current Lamborghini camp, this most iconic legendary model inherits the powerful genes of the brand's classic S-Class and further develops and evolves into the new Aventador S Coupé. The Aventador S Coupé models have Lamborghini's highly recognizable design language and match the 740-horsepower V12 naturally aspirated engine. These are just a glimpse of its strong lineup; the new Lamborghini LDVA (Lamborghini Active Vehicle Dynamics) dynamic control system provides an unparalleled driving experience for car drivers who are admiring themselves and constantly seeking challenges.

Passion

The new Aventador S Coupé model inherits the outstanding performance of the Aventador family and matches the outstanding performance of the V12 naturally aspirated engine. Appearance Design

The design of Aventador S Coupé was focused on how to strengthen the characteristics of the Aventador family, not change. Aventador S Coupé exhibited the highly recognizable design genes of the Lamborghini car, while at the same time paying more attention to dynamic, refined and aggressive design expressions.
