History of Lamborghini

Automobili Lamborghini SpA is an Italian car manufacturer, one of the world's top sports car makers and one of the European luxury brands. The company is located in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy, by Ferruccio. Lamborghini was founded in 1963. Lamborghini suffered from bankruptcy in 1980 due to poor management; after several changes, it was owned by Audi in 1998 and is now part of the Volkswagen Group. Lamborghini's logo is a bull power that is full of strength and is attacking each other.
